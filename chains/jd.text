Job Description: Medical Representative – Januvia Division

Company: MSD (Merck Sharp & Dohme)
Division: Diabetes Care (Januvia – Sitagliptin)

🎯 Role Summary

We are looking for a dynamic and result-oriented Medical Representative to promote MSD’s anti-diabetic portfolio, primarily Januvia (Sitagliptin) and related combination therapies, to healthcare professionals. The role focuses on driving prescription generation, building strong doctor relationships, and achieving sales targets within the assigned territory.

🧠 Key Responsibilities
👨‍⚕️ Doctor & Healthcare Engagement
Visit doctors (General Physicians, Diabetologists, Endocrinologists) regularly
Promote Januvia and related diabetes portfolio
Conduct scientific detailing of product benefits, clinical data, and guidelines
Build and maintain strong professional relationships with key prescribers
📈 Sales & Target Achievement
Achieve monthly/quarterly sales and prescription targets
Monitor territory performance and market share growth
Identify high-potential doctors and convert them into prescribers
📊 Market Intelligence
Track competitor activities (e.g., DPP4 inhibitors, SGLT2 inhibitors)
Provide feedback on market trends, patient behavior, and competitor strategies
Report field insights to Area Sales Manager (ASM)
📚 Scientific Promotion
Organize CMEs, doctor meetings, and product presentations
Ensure ethical promotion aligned with compliance guidelines
Stay updated with latest diabetes management guidelines
🗂️ Reporting & Compliance
Maintain accurate daily call reports (DCR)
Ensure proper documentation of doctor visits and sales activities
Follow company SOPs and ethical marketing standards
🎓 Required Qualifications
B.Pharm / D.Pharm / B.Sc / Life Sciences (preferred)
Strong understanding of diabetes therapy is an advantage
Freshers or 0–3 years experience in pharma sales
💡 Skills Required
Excellent communication and persuasion skills
Strong scientific understanding ability
Relationship-building mindset
Target-driven and self-motivated
Territory planning & execution
📍 Work Requirements
Field-based role (doctor visits in assigned territory)
Daily travel required within city/region
Two-wheeler mandatory in most locations
🚀 Performance Metrics (KPI)
Prescription growth (Januvia + combos)
Doctor coverage and conversion rate
Monthly sales achievement
Market share improvement in territory
